Privacy Policy (Afghan Currency Converter App)


1. WHAT IS THE APP?

Afghan Currency Converter App is an application that developed in Flutter Framework to shows the latest currency information for Afghans people and also available both on Android and IOS devices.

This App includes these features:

— Shows the latest currency information per day.

— Shows the app in both light mode and dark mode.

— Convert foreign country currencies to AFN and reverse.

— Change to locale language.

— Has offline mode functionality.

— Get notifications.

— Sharable with your friends.

2. WHERE DO WE GET THE DATA?

We get the currency information from Da Afghanistan Bank(dab.gov.af) website and shows in the app with converter functionality.
